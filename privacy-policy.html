1. Introduction

AR Drawing Sketchflow ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining transparency about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or share any personal data directly. However, the App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain information automatically, such as:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location data (for ad relevance)
  • Ad interaction data (e.g., impressions, clicks)
  • Device information (model, operating system version)

This information is processed by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy. We do not have access to or control over this data.

3. Permissions Used

The App requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Camera: To capture photos and videos during drawing sessions using the camera overlay feature.
  • Storage (Gallery access): To save captured media, import user images for tracing, and access the line art converter feature.
  • Internet access: To display AdMob advertisements within the app.

Important: No photos, videos, or other media files are uploaded to any server. All media remains stored locally on your device and under your complete control.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display the following types of advertisements:

  • Banner ads
  • Interstitial ads (full-screen ads shown between activities)
  • Native advanced ads

AdMob may use cookies or device identifiers for personalized advertising and performance tracking. These ads help us keep the app free while maintaining minimal interruptions to your drawing experience.

You can opt out of ad personalization by:

  • Visiting Google Ads Settings
  • Adjusting your device's ad settings (iOS: Settings > Privacy > Advertising; Android: Settings > Google > Ads)

5. Children's Privacy

AR Drawing Sketchflow is intended for general audiences and is not specifically directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at the email address below, and we will take steps to remove such information.

6. Data Security

The App does not collect or store personal data on external servers. All files and captured media remain on the user's device. We take reasonable measures to ensure the security of the app, but please note:

  • You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device
  • We recommend using device lock screens and security features
  • Backup your important drawings and media files regularly

7. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. View their Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services: For app functionality and updates

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store your personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. However, you have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Uninstall the app at any time
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, regulatory, or operational reasons. Any updates will appear on this page with a revised "Last updated" date at the top.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
